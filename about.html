About CGPA Booster

CGPA Booster is a cozy local academic workspace built to organize documents and estimate study targets.

Workspace Modules

Metrics Dashboard

Central stats detailing subjects, study notes folders, and target benchmarks.

GPA Estimators

Estimates credit-weighted GPA ratios and conversions to equivalent percentages.

Attendance Safety

Computes Safety metrics and successive classes needed using instant counters.

Workspace File Indexer

Scans notes subfolders dynamically, linking PDFs to read online or download.
