Calculation Formulas

CGPA is computed by multiplying the credits of each course by the grade point earned, summing them up, and dividing by total credit points.

  • ✓ Credits serve as weights.
  • ✓ Formula: CGPA = sum(Credits × Points) / sum(Credits)
  • ✓ Percentage Estimate = CGPA × 9.5
